SERVICES

HOME   |   SERVICES

  Veterinary Consultation

Our experienced veterinarians provide detailed health checkups, accurate diagnosis, and effective treatment plans to ensure your pet’s overall well-being, early disease detection, and long, healthy life.

  Vaccination Services

We offer complete vaccination programs based on your pet’s age and breed, protecting them from serious infectious diseases while maintaining proper medical records and reminders for future doses.

  Pet Grooming

Our professional grooming services include bathing, coat trimming, nail clipping, ear cleaning, and hygiene care, helping your pet stay clean, comfortable, confident, and free from skin issues.

  Emergency Care

Our emergency care service provides immediate medical attention during accidents, sudden illnesses, or injuries, ensuring fast response, expert handling, and critical support when your pet needs it most.

  Pet Boarding

We provide safe, hygienic, and comfortable boarding facilities with constant supervision, proper feeding schedules, and loving care so your pet feels secure and stress-free while you are away.

  Nutrition & Diet Planning

We create personalized diet plans based on your pet’s breed, age, activity level, and medical needs, ensuring balanced nutrition that supports growth, immunity, digestion, and overall health.

  Pet Adoption Services

We connect loving families with pets in need through a responsible adoption process, offering guidance, background checks, and post-adoption support to ensure lifelong happiness for both.

  Home Visit Services

Our home visit services bring veterinary consultations, grooming, and basic treatments directly to your doorstep, reducing travel stress for pets and providing comfort and convenience for owners.

  Parasite Control

Our parasite control services prevent and treat ticks, fleas, and internal worms, protecting your pet from infections, discomfort, and long-term health complications caused by parasites.

  Dental Care

We provide comprehensive dental care including oral examinations, teeth cleaning, and gum treatments to prevent bad breath, infections, and tooth loss, ensuring better overall health for your pet.

  Pet Insurance Assistance

We help pet owners understand and choose the right insurance plans, offering guidance on coverage options, claim processes, and financial protection against unexpected medical emergencies.

  Behavioral Consultation

Our behavior specialists identify the root causes of anxiety, aggression, or stress in pets and provide practical solutions to create a calm, balanced, and happy living environment.
